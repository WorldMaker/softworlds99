The Advantages of Playing SoftWorlds Games

What are the Advantages of playing a SoftWorlds distributed game?

Here are some of the advantages:

  • You are assured a quality game. This doesn't mean that the game will use all the latest hardware and have full color animation with hot fmv, this means that the program will be enjoyable and worth playing.
  • You are given full contact and help information, as well as bug report addresses and a place to find up to date information on patches, fixes and more.
  • Stuck? We will provide complete hint service, ranging from our tips network (not yet complete) to a dedicated pay-per hint system (also not yet available).
  • Played all of our best and want more? Want to develop your own games? We will provide the resources you need (engine, sub-engines) to get started on your own game, and we will offer you the benefits of working through SoftWorlds as your distributor.
