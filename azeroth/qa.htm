What is the game about?

The game is about Azeroth, a paradise, a place where unicorns and dragons and cute, cuddly 6 foot rats were just a few of the wonders and delights to sample, a place that had been made a resort to the richest aliens, a place where wounds were healed and true joy was realised.

Who is the game about?

The game is about Bob, one of the last to escape Azeroth's fate, he became the richest man in the Tri-Galactic area.

Who are Jutnix Jare and First Volus Ortrius?

First Volus Ortrius was the founder of the Guild of Catalogs, the head of tri-galactic research. The Volus are researchers who stretch through time and space learning and cataloging the world.

Jutnix Jare was a reporter for the Galactinet Texbloid, the Tri-Galactic News Network, he quit after becoming friends with Bob, and he wrote Bob's life story. Most of what the Tri-Galactic Area knows about Bob is through Jutnix.

Why do some people have one name and others have two?

Actually, everybody has two, but Volis drop there second ones when they are Apprenticed. Noboddy knows Bob's second name, but many have guesses.

What is a Texbloid?

A Texbloid is a magazine that resides on the Galactinet and can be read by anyone.

What is the Galactinet?

The Galactinet is a unique network which binds all technology at the quantum level. The problem is not in placing your information up, but being able to read the data that is already there. Most people use an Integrator Service Provider (ISP) to read the data, the Volis use their own server, which has the tri-galaxy's biggest Integrator.

What species live in the Tri-Galactic Area?

Humans and animals mostly, because Azeroth lies in what is called the human disaster area (hda) which is the section of the tree in which Azeroth (or its equivalant) is destroyed, Azeroth is destroyed in the middle of all the other worlds, and thus forms the middle line or base/trunk line. On all the Earths in this sector (which is the only sector easily travellable, and thus, GPS coordinates refer solely to this sector), the humans manage to escape their fate, and even manage to gain the high hand in Tri-Galactic politics. The Tri-Galactic area refers to the three Galaxies which support humans in every sector. There are things outside of the Tri-Galactic area, but humans don't really care.
