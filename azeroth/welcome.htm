Welcome, and let me introduce you to Azeroth: Legacy, a game so large that the famous Galactinet Texbloid, "How big is big?" barely scratches the surface. Yes, Azeroth is set in one of the largest science fiction multiverses, mine. It will serve as a meeting place, a place to explore, and a place to "live". The first story in this universe was released to DRAGONzine in the June issue (Stereotypes) and it won't be the last, in fact, as Azeroth (and its previous incarnation Eazer) grow, the exploits and adventures of its players will be intertwined into the tales, and will influence events in the Tri-Galactic area.
