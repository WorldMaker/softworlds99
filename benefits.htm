Benefits from working with SoftWorlds:

  • We handle all your distribution and advertising. We will submit you to freeware/shareware sites like Download.Com, Hotfiles.Com, FreeGames.org and others.
  • We will build and host your web pages so that you can concentrate on your games.
  • We will handle all Press Releases and other junk so you don't have to.
  • We will act as your liason to Publishers and Players alike, we can provide your players with Tech Support, Hints and more.
  • We will let you make use of SoftWorld's Customizable Navigation bar for your own pages... Information on Request.
  • Games we distribute will be given FREE advertising space on our Banner Network and our special Button Network.
  • We help you communicate with other of our teams to help you get your game finished.

Requirements for working with SoftWorlds:

  • We want you to place one of our full screen logos at the start of your game.
  • We want you to credit us in your credits for Distribution.
  • We would like you to add the phrase "A Satellite of SoftWorlds" or our Satellite logo. (Will be provided on request)
  • We must assure quality to our Players, and thus you must agree to let us test all of your program for free and if we deem necessary discontinue our liscence because of lack of quality.
  • We ask a small 25% distribution royalty on all shareware games. If you sell 100 copies at $20 we will receive $500 and you will receive $1,500.
